EX-FRONTEND DEVELOPER & GRAPHIC DESIGNER

I am AYA REYAD, a former Frontend Developer and a Graphic Designer with a Bachelor's degree in Plastic Arts, specialized in Oil Painting.

MY VISION
 To bridge the worlds of art and technology by creating visuals and digital experiences that feel both precise and deeply expressive. Coming from a fine arts background, I bring a painter’s eye for detail, while my frontend development journey strengthened my appreciation for clean structure, tidy logic, and precision in numbers.

MY MISSION
 To combine the emotional depth of oil painting, the visual storytelling of graphic design, and the organized, methodical nature of frontend development into work that is balanced, thoughtful, and impactful. My goal is to craft designs and experiences that are not only beautiful but also functional, intuitive, and built with intention.
